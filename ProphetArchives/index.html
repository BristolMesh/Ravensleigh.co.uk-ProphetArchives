Overview

I am a proud, patriotic Rising member, a Regent, but I carry that pride with humility. Through battles and attacks against the bloodline, including being doxxed and targeted by the Kaddins, I faltered. I lost my way at times and became someone I regret.

In those moments, I looked up to Kalevos. His strength, restraint, and example reminded me of who I was meant to be and inspired me to return to my values.

I have never doxxed anyone, nor have I condoned such acts. This is who I am — a Patriotic Rising member until the end, shaped by failure and guided by those I admire.
